**Introduction** 
The Demo Engineering job family is responsible for understanding customer challenges and translating them into technical solutions and demonstrations. They craft experiences for customers that make complex technology feel intuitive, powerful and essential. They’re responsible for designing, building, maintaining, and evolving high-impact product demonstrations that showcase the company’s AI-powered marketing technology in a way that is relevant to customer use cases, technically accurate, and persuasive. The team ensures demos reflect real-world data, AI workflows, and measurable outcomes, while staying aligned with product roadmap, go-to-market strategy, and customer needs.
